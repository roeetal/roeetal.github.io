About me

I created this website not only because I wanted to learn a bit of web development but also because I have a deep interest in technology, and wanted to extend my passion to family and friends. So the way I see the world is as follows: Technology is this relaively new awesome thing that can do so much good for the world. And here is where my personality begins to diverge.

There is a part of me that is always thinking of whats next. I like to think of little innovations that could do really epic or helpful things for people. This part of me also likes abstract mathematics and modern physics and is always wanting to learn more of them. I like to image that one day I could be one of the people who work on an innovation of the future, like the personal quantum computer.

But then there is the other part of me. Now this part like to think of us, humans. How we think; why we do the things that we do, whether on an individual level or a social level. In my ideal world, I will spend the next couple of years working on the aforementioned. I'd study theory from mathemtics to physics to neuroscience, while doing practical engineering things. Hopefully the content on this website will aptly communicate how my plan is going.
