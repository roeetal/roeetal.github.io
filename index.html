What is Orbit?

UBC Orbit is an engineering design team at the University of British Columbia in Vancouver.

I joined the Command and Data Handling team during my first semester at UBC. Here is a bit about the work we do.

Caution! Construction below!

This is a Solidworks render of our Thunderbird satellite.

It was modelled by the structure team in solidworks. Unfortunately, I do not have a good render yet to make any cool animations and renders of the satellite.



The satellite is designed for early forrest fire detection in British Columbia. We are entered in the Canadian Satellite Design Challenge. We hope to win, so we can launch our satellite into orbit and prevent forrest fires!
