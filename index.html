Thunderbird: Our current mission will put a constellation of six cube satellites, each equipped with artificially intelligent imaging capabilities, in low-earth orbit. They will continuously image the globe and act as an early warning system for forrest fires everywhere. Visit our website or check out our open source contributions.

CDH & Trillium

Control and Data Handling

Trillium is a redundant MCU system which uses three STM32 boards to process data while simultaneously verifying each other's processes. It's main purpose is to reduce the risk of bit flips and other potential radiation hazards in space. I spend the last two semesters developing and testing Trillium. Next, I will work on making the operating system for it.

Automated Claw

An APSC 101 Class Project

We were tasked with building an automated claw that could simulate retrieving debris from the bottom of the ocean. Built using an Arduino, a servo motor, a sonar and various workshop tools.

Apartment Bot

Made with Sam Ingersoll

Why spend time searching the web for things like apartments when a bot can do that for you. This bot scrapes Craigslist for new apartments listings, uses the Google Maps Distance API to filter results based on location, and then updates us with suitable housing options on Slack. If you are interested, here is the Github repo.

About me

I created this website not only because I wanted to learn a bit of web development but also because I have a deep interest in technology and wanted to extend my passion and experiences to family and friends.

There is a part of me that is always thinking of new technological innovations. I often like to try come up with my own ideas that could allow people to do really epic things, whatever they may be. This part of me also likes abstract mathematics and physics and is always wanting to learn more about each them. My dream is to work on an innovation of the future, like a 'personal quantum computer' for example.

There is also this other part of me: and this part of me like to think of us, humans. How we think; why we do the things that we do; how we behave as individuals and as communities. In my ideal world, I will spend the next couple of years working on the aforementioned, and maybe even try merge the worlds of mathematics and physics with that of philosophy. Hopefully the content on this website will aptly communicate how my journey is going...
